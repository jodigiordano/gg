Guide

In gg, you add boxes icon-box and lists icon-list to the canvas and then you create links between them with lines icon-link.

General operations
icon-undo Undo an operation.
icon-redo Redo an operation.
icon-move

Select one or many boxes, lines, etc. in the canvas.

Move one or many boxes, lines, etc. in the canvas.

Move one or many boxes inside other boxes.

Move a line between boxes.
icon-eraser Remove one or many boxes, lines, etc. from the canvas.
icon-text Add or modify the title of a box, list, line, etc.
icon-paint Paint a box, a list, a line, etc.
icon-trash Remove one or many selected boxes, lists, etc.
icon-duplicate Duplicate one or many selected boxes, lists, etc.
Operations on boxes & lists
icon-box Add a box to the canvas.
icon-list Add a list to the canvas.
Operations on lines
icon-link

Select an existing line to change its properties.

Add a new line between two boxes, lists, etc.

Hold your pointer on the first box then drag your pointer to the second box to create the line.

Operations on canvas
icon-zoom-fit Fit the camera to show all the content of the canvas.
icon-zoom-in Zoom in the canvas.
icon-zoom-out Zoom out of the canvas.
icon-pan Pan the camera in the canvas.

About

gg aims to help you quickly produce stunning diagrams, by letting you focus on its components instead of fiddling with its layout.

For any question, you can contact me at jodi@gg-charts.com or create an issue in Github.

Enjoy!

Privacy

gg value your time and your privacy.

As a guest of this service:

  • - There are no ads.
  • - There are no analytics.
  • - There are no cookies.
  • - There are no PII collected.

100% of your data is kept in your browser and is never collected.

As a member of gg+, the cloud offering:

gg tries very hard to collect as few information as possible to operate the service.

When you sign up, you are agreeing to the Terms of Use and the Privacy Policy.
